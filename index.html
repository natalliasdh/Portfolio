About Me

I moved to the United States from Minsk, Belarus, in 2017. Currently I work part-time as an Internet Analyst. My job includes evaluation of search engine results and web page quality, which I find really interesting.

My working experience includes managing the Internet Technology department of a TV company, where I performed mostly organizational functions. I have always been fascinated with the process of web development and how dynamic this field is. I decided to pursue my interest and enrolled in Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp.

I am an industrious person, and I always put my heart and responsibility into my work.
